An officer, a contractor, and a journalist: How the Corona crisis is being managed in Egypt?

The Corona crisis in Egypt shows a deeper governance crisis. Egypt has known for months how dangerous the Corona virus can be, but only decided to close the borders and implement a quarantine when two generals died of Corona virus, after contact with engineers from a Chinese company who have been working on the new capital project. When the Egyptian president received suggestions that a quarantine should be implemented from 3 p.m. and afterwards, he immediately rejected this suggestion, and was accompanied by the media and notable businessmen. Egyptian media continues to play an important role in slowing down the response to Corona. But now, Egypt is facing a serious economic crisis which will exacerbate if the quarantine is not lifted. This will lead the parliament to meet and discuss how to combat “Coronaphobia” and the conspiracy to undermine the Egyptian economy. If this regime has spent its effort on combating Corona rather than protecting the regime, it would have gotten rid of the virus a month ago. So Egypt is infested with a governance problem . Its problem is not how to deal with Corona but rather how to treat itself from a chronic Virus that has lasted for 10 years, which is getting rid of Sisi… from the inside.
