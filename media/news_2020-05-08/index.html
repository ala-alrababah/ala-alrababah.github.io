Because it is worthy

The meeting of President Bashar al-Assad yesterday with the governmental group concerned with confronting the Corona pandemic and its repercussions was important in terms of reassuring the Syrians that the Syrian state is aware of the difficulties facing them in light of the spread of the Corona pandemic.

The president accurately and logically described the reality and spoke with transparency and honesty about the difficulties that Syrians are experiencing. The meeting also proposed a set of effective and decisive solutiosn to everything that concerns Syrians during this period.

The president asserted the importance of proposing non-traditional solutions that fit the current crisis and that are different from previous solutions. The president also asked the group to propose laws and changes to legislation in roder to increase the severity of punishments to traders and monopolizers who are leading to higher prices.

Perhaps the most important message is that the Syrian state clearly realizes the situation and is able to take solutions to protect its citizens. For this, we repeat: let us trust the state as it has continued to prove that it is worthy of our trust.
